**Optional, but recommended.** Square aspect ratio, ideally 512×512. PNG, JPG, or GIF.

## How it's displayed

- **In chat**, cropped to a circle
- **On the home page card**, shown as the full square

Because of the circle crop, anything important should be near the center of the image. Don't put text or key details near the corners.

## Uploading vs generating

You have two options:

- **Upload**, bring your own image. Useful if you have a specific look in mind, fan art, or a photo.
- **Generate**, describe the character and Sutorichat creates an avatar for you. Quick, and the avatar will naturally match the visual style you want for image-generating characters.

<Tip>
  Even if your character will generate images during chat, the avatar is a separate field. It's not regenerated each turn, it's a fixed portrait. Pick one that captures the character's signature look.
</Tip>

## Does the avatar change behavior?

No. The avatar is purely visual, the system doesn't "see" it when generating responses. So if your avatar shows a wizard but your greeting describes a chef, the character will act like a chef.

## Moderation

Avatars are run through moderation filters. If your upload violates the content policy, it'll be rejected. Generated avatars are pre-filtered.

<Warning>
  Don't use copyrighted celebrity photos, watermarked images you don't have rights to, or anything that could be confused with a real living person without consent.
</Warning>
